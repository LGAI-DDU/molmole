MolMole: Molecule Mining from Scientific Literature

First Author*, Second Author*, Third Author
LG AI Research
*Indicates Equal Contribution
Paper Data

Abstract

The extraction of molecular structures and reaction data from scientific documents is challenging due to their varied, unstructured chemical formats and complex document layouts. We introduce MolMole, a vision-based deep learning frame- work that combines molecule detection, reaction diagram parsing, optical chem- ical structure recognition (OCSR), and an interactive editing tool into a unified pipeline, designed to automate the extraction of chemical data directly from page- level documents. To address the absence of page-level benchmark and evalua- tion metric, we present a test set of 550 pages annotated with molecule bounding boxes, reaction labels, and MOL files, accompanied by a novel evaluation met- ric. Experimental results demonstrate that MolMole surpasses existing toolkits in both accuracy and efficiency on our new benchmark dataset. The MolMole project page and our test set can be found at https://MOLMOLE.ai.
